Fried Potatoes and Hot Dogs

Fried Potatoes and Hot Dogs, my mamo would fix regularly. I think it was a meal stretcher - she'd throw in this economic meal in the middle of the week, open up a can of pork and beans or corn and we had a tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium sized potatoes sliced thin
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 1 package of hot dogs (Sliced)
  • 1 Stick of Butter

Directions

  1. Place onions, potatoes and butter in a skillet, turn to medium heat and fry until the potatoes are well done.
  2. Add sliced hot dogs to the mix until heated through.
