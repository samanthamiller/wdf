Ingredients

  • 6 medium sized potatoes sliced thin
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 1 package of hot dogs (Sliced)
  • 1 Stick of Butter

Directions

* Cook times may vary pending on gas or electric stoves

  1. Place onions, potatoes and butter in a skillet, turn to medium heat and fry until the potatoes are well done.
  2. Add sliced hot dogs to the mix until heated through.
